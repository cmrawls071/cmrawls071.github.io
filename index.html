Latitude and Weather

An exploration of how latitude affects various weather patterns around the planet.

Project Summary

The purpose of this project was to identify how various weather patterns, such as temperature, humidity, and cloudiness, change as latitude gets further from, or closer to, the Equator. To accomplish this, a script was created to generate a randomized list of 500+ cities. The data on these cities was then pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API.
